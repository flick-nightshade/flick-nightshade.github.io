All About Self Care

By Felicity McKee

Why is Self Care worthwhile?

Self care is important, you deserve to take time for yourelf. In this fast paced world, it can be hard to stop and just embace the present moment. Self Care helps you to STOP and embrace the here and now, so you can better face the future

You can find Self Care Starter Kit thanks to the Blurt It Foundation, which focus on self care for mental health. It is a good starting point - of course there will be more on this site too!

It can seem weird or diffuicult to set time aside just for you, but you're worth it. Take it in small steps, and eventually you will find it easier to include it in your everyday practice and notice increased mindfulness and ability to be the best version of you that you can be!

Does Self Care only help me?

Self care is not only about considering our needs; it is rather about knowing what we need to do in order to take care of ourselves, being subsequently, able to take care of others as well. That is, if I don’t take enough care of myself, I won’t be in the place to give to my loved ones either.
