A little about me...

I'm 25, a feminist and activist for mental health awareness and disability rights

I've been chronically ill for 8+ years now and set up an organisation called Chronically Fabulous to help challenge perceptions fo what it means to be ill and raise awareness of a variety of conditions

When not busy organising things at Chron Fab, i'm studying at Queens University Belfast, and I am a final year Social Anthropology Student as well as the current Ms Ireland 2017

I enjoy learning new things, hence why i'm trying to learn how to code right now, and the proof is on this webpage. I love all things geeky, from Sherlock, Marvel and Star Trek
